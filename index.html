About

Welcome to this somewhat personal blog. I am by no means an expert on music, however I do have a background, as a musician, having classical training on piano, being self-taught as a guitarist, and studying basic music theory. Here I share my thoughts and analysis of particular songs, using what I've learned about music, as well as my own thoughts and opinions. I just want to share the music that I have grown to love, with you all, and I hope that you will find some music that you will like on here too. Happy reading!
